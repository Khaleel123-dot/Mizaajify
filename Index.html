Mizaj(Temperament) Definition

Mizaj refers to the natural quality or state resulting from the unique combination and interaction of the four humors -- blood (Dam), yellow bile (Safra), phlegm (Balgham), and black bile (Sauda) -- within the human body. This temperament influences an individual's physical health, mental state, emotional behavior, and overall personality.
